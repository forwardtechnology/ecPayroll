Example – Update timesheet (eRoster)

 

Example

Updated timesheet result change change 3 Jan 2012 with Late 30 minutes to 0 minutes

 

 

Steps

1.            Click [Timesheet]

 

2.            Select [Department]

 

3.            Click the [icon] of Staff A0033 for details results.

 

4.            Click the [icon] on 3 Jan 2012 for adjustment

 

5.            Adjust the Late minute from 30 minute to 0 minute, and then click [Update] to confirm this changes.
