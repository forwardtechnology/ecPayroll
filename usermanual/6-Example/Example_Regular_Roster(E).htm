Steps

1.            Click [Duty Group] and select [Add new group]

 

2.            Enter 9-18 (A) Group on the [Description

 

3.            Press [Save]

 

4.            Go back to main screen, right click 9-18 (A) Group and click [Timetable Setup]

 

5.            Right click duty rule row (Any one displayed calendar date) and click [Duty Arrangement Setup]

 

6.            Click [Add Rule]

 

7.            Choose [ 9 – 18] (pre-set duty)

 

8.            Choose [M, T, W, T, F] (mean Mon, Tue, Wed, Thu, Fri)

 

9.            Click [Add Rule] again

 

10.         Choose [9-13] and choose [S] (The S on right hand side means Sat)

 

11.         Enter 2012/01/04 on [Effective Date]

 

12.         Enter 2 on [Repeat per week]

 


13.         Click [Add Rule] again

 

14.         Choose [OFF], enter 2012/01/14 on [Effective Date], choose [S] (The S on right hand side means Sat) and enter 2 on [Repeat per week]

 

15.         Click [Add Rule] again

 

16.         Choose [OFF] and choose [S] (The S on left hand side means Sun)

 

17.         Press [Save]

 

18.         Press [Yes]
