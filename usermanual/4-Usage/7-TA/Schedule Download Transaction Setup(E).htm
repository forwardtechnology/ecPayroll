Schedule Download Transaction Setup

Make sure you have completed session(s)

l            Reader Setup

 

Steps

1            Starting Transaction Download Server

 

2          Click  on the toolbar to set schedule download

 

 

3          Tick the Schedule download checkbox.

 

4          Select one of four available methods to activate Schedule Download:

4.1         Monthly: transaction will be downloaded on the first day of every month.

4.2         Weekly: transaction will be downloaded at AM12:00 every weekday.

4.3         Everyday: transaction will be downloaded at the specific time everyday.

4.4         On every n minute(s): transaction will be downloaded on every n minute.

 

5          Click  to save and activate the setting.

 

6          After setting is saved, the next activation time will be shown on the bottom of the Download Manager window:

After:

 

7          When the scheduled time is met, Download Manager will download the transaction from reader automatically.
