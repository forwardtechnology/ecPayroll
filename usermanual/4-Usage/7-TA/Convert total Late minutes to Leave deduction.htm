Convert total Late minutes to Leave deduction

Make sure you have completed following session(s)

l   View Attendance Result

 

 

Depends on your company policy, if you want to deduct Leave balance by calculating total of Late minutes, you can simply do it by following below steps:

 

1.          Click the [Commander] button from the main screen toolbar

 

 

 

2.          Click “Late minutes to Leave deduction” from the menu on the left hand side.

 

[screen here]

 

3.          Select the date range for calculate total late minutes.

 

4.          Select a Leave type to be deduct.

 

5.          Enter a remark if any. This remark will be shown in the Leave deduction record.

 

6.          Select which employee(s) you want to perform the conversion. Click the [EE Filter] button to select employee(s).

 

 

 

CAUTION

Do check very carefully the selected options especially the selected employees. Please note that once the process is executed, you cannot undo the process by batch.

 

       

 

7.          Click the [Execute] button to start conversion.

 

To confirm the action, enter “EXECUTE” and press the [OK] button to start running the process.

 

 

8.          Finish. Now, in the screen Employee > Leave record, you may find the Leave balance was deducted with a new Leave deduction record.
