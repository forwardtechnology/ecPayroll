Export Single Payslip

Make sure you have completed following session(s)

l   Add Salary Record

 

 

Instead of printing payslips for all staffs, you may want to view a particular payslip quickly. It can be done by simply export the payslip to MS Excel.

 

Steps

1          Open the salary record to be exported. Please refer to the manual “View Salary Record” for details

 

 

2          Click the [Export] button to export payslip to MS EXCEL

 

 

3          Finish
