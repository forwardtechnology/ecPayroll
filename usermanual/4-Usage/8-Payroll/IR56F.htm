Generating IR56F Tax form

Make sure you have completed following session(s)

l   How ecPayroll system handles HK taxation

l   Add New Payroll Record

 

 

In Hong Kong, employers are required to submit the tax information of the staffs who are about to be resigned, to government 1 month prior to the cessation of employment. For details, please refer to IRD web page. It can be done by printing the IR56F report and send to the Inland Revenue Department (IRD) by post or delivery in person. Before that, you must get the approval from IRD to submit computerized IR56F form. See the diagram below.
