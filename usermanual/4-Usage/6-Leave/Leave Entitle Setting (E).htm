What is Leave Entitle Plan

A Leave entitle Plan is a group of settings about how to entitle Leave days (or hour/minute) to employees automatically. In our system, you may create more than 1 Leave entitle plan depends on your company policy. At least 1 Leave entitle plan should be created for each Leave Type which you want the system to auto calculate and entitle leave days to employees. Sometimes, say Annual Leave, if your company has 2 entitle policy for different group of employees (e.g. General staffs and Manager), you should create 2 Leave entitle plan for them.

 

In each Leave entitle plan, it include 5 main components, and they are:

 

 

Brief explanation

Period

Defines how long as a period. Depends on Leave type, the period should be different. For example, the period of Annual Leave (AL) should be a year, the period of Pay Sick Leave (PSL) should be a month, the period of Compensation Leave (CL) may also be a month if it turns monthly total OT hours into CL.

Calculation

Defines how many days (or hours) to be entitled. Again, depends on Leave Type, the calculation is different. For example, Annual Leave should be calculated on service year, while Pay Sick Leave should be fixed. Compensation Leave should be auto calculate total OT hours. Rest day may depends on how many Sunday within the month.

Entitle style

Defines when to entitle the calculated Leave days to staffs. For example, Staff A will be entitled 12 annual leave days per year.  Depends on your company policy, these 12 days can be entitled at the beginning of the year, year end, or even prorated day by day within the year.

Expiry

Defines when to auto forfeit the remain Leave days. You may make it forfeit after certain periods or months. You can also define how many days can be carry forward to next period (year).

Balance

Defines how to handle Leave balance. You can define the ceiling of the Leave balance during the period, or carry forward to next period.
