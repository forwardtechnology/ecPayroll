What is Credential

Sometimes, employees are not allowed to work without some kind of licenses, certificates, or permits. Credential normally refer to the records of those licenses, such as Driving license, Electrical Engineering License, Construction Industry Safety Training Certificate, etc. For each credential record, you may record the details such as the credential type, issued by which organization, the effective period, and a free text box for any text remark. Please follow below steps to add new credential record.
