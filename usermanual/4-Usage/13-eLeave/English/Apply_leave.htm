Usage of e-Leave Module

Calendar View

Leave Application

1.          Press the [Calendar View] button from the menu

 

2.          Select a day and time section for Leave Application

 

3.          Press the [Leave Apply] to continue

 

4.          Select [Leave Type] and enter [Reason]

 

5.          Press [Submit] to submit

 

6.          Go to [Calendar View] again, you will see your leave application status [Waiting Approval]

 

7.          You may check the detail by pressing the [Waiting Approval]

 

         

                             P.S.: Press [Remove] in case of cancel leave application
