How to Copy Salary Profile

Make sure you have completed following session(s)

l            Already setup salary profile

 

Steps

 

1.          Press “Payroll Maintenance”

 

 

2.          Choose “Salary Profile” and select one of the salary profiles which you want to copy

 

3.     Press “Copy”

 

 

4.     Press “New” and open a new salary profile

 

 

5.     Press “Paste” and enter the new salary profile name. Press “Update” to store the profile.
